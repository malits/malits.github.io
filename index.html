Hi! I'm Max, currently a third-year undergraduate student and researcher at UC Berkeley. At the moment, my interests concern Natural Language Processing and Optimization, two fields which I have the great fortune of working in under Professor Zachary Pardos and Professor Miki Lustig. I am also interested in the ethics of Artificial Intelligence, specifically the increased scrutiny and regulation of reinforcement learning algorithms that interact with human beings. Currently, I am investigating the use of large language models like BERT to analyze student short-answer responses, and developing Python wrappers for high-performance iterative optimization algorithms written in C. My other hobbies include running, reading, taking too many math classes for my own good, and organizing concerts.
